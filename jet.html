Jet – The Stealth Fighter

Jet doesn’t walk. Jet only has two modes: asleep and Mach 3. He can go from “completely invisible” to “launching off the bookshelf” in under half a second, usually while someone is holding a full cup of coffee.

His favorite game is “ambush the ankles” – a tactical operation where he hides around a corner, waits for footsteps, and then executes a perfectly timed sideways jump that startles everyone, including himself.

Jet’s Flight Log

- Logged 327 successful zoomies across the hallway runway.
- Performed 19 unauthorized landings on keyboards during critical work calls.
- Claims all cardboard boxes as aircraft carriers.
