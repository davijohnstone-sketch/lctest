Rollino – The Cardboard High Roller

Rollino doesn’t gamble with money, he gambles with dignity. His favorite move is to dramatically roll off the couch, land upside down, and pretend it was all part of an advanced stretching routine.

His true obsession is cardboard. Shoe boxes, delivery boxes, flattened packing boxes – all are part of his private VIP lounge. Entry is by invitation only, and the only accepted currency is treats.

Rollino’s House Games

- “Will the human trip over me?” – 50/50 odds, he’s fine either way.
- “Can I fit in that box?” – The answer is always “Yes” in his mind.
- “If I knock this off the table, will someone react?” – Progressive jackpot.
